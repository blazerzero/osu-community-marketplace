What is OSU Community Marketplace?

The OSU Community Marketplace is an all-in-one hub for members of the OSU community to buy and sell products and services, as well as share housing availabilities, exclusively to other OSU community members.

We know that it's important to find that balance of advertising to a large audience while being able to verify who's seeing your listings.

So by authenticating users through their ONID accounts, you can be assured that everyone viewing your listings on the marketplace is a verified part of our extensive community here at Oregon State University.
